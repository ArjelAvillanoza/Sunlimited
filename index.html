Our Story

Who We Are

SunLimited was founded with a simple mission: to make clean energy accessible to everyone. Today, we continue to help homes and businesses reduce their carbon footprint and energy costs.

As an official Sungrow distributor based in Tarlac City, we bring world-class solar technology to Central Luzon and beyond — backed by expert installation and genuine after-sales care.
